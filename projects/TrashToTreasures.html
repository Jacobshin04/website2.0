Built with React, Next.js, and Tailwind CSS for a seamless UI, with a Node.js and MongoDB backend supporting AI-powered upcycling suggestions and community voting.

TrashToTreasure is a sustainable showcase platform where users upload images of packaging waste and receive AI-generated upcycling ideas. After transforming the waste into creative or useful items, users share their results, and the community votes on the best upcycled creations, promoting sustainability and innovation.
